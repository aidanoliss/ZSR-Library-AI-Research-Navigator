# Investment Memo Prompt

Write a one-page memo for human diligence. Do not provide financial advice or investment instructions.

## Required Sections

- Company
- Domain
- What it does
- Why it matters
- Why now
- Founder signal
- Market signal
- Product signal
- Traction signal
- Risks
- Diligence questions
- Recommendation
- Confidence level

## Evidence Rules

Separate:

- Facts directly supported by sources
- Model inferences
- Unknowns
- Risks
- Confidence level

Never fabricate funding, revenue, customers, investors, or founder credentials.
